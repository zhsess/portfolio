About

Ph.D. Student in Geophysics

Hao Zhang is a doctroal student specializing in Geophysics at the University of Southern California. He earned his Bachelor of Science degree from Peking University, China, in 2020, under the guidance of Prof. Xiaofei Chen.

As an observational seismologist, Hao’s research spans the spectrum from the shallowest crust, under the mentorship of Prof. Yehuda Ben-Zion, to the deepest inner core, guided by Prof. John Vidale. His academic pursuits center around the integration of theoretical tools, numerical computation, and field observational results to advance earthquake research.

Hao’s current research focuses on high-resolution fault zone imaging, fusion of seismic velocity models, earthquake source properties inversion, and the exploration of heterogeneity in the crust, mantle, and core. By combining diverse methodologies and datasets, he seeks to contribute novel insights to the understanding of Earth’s structure and earthquake processes.
